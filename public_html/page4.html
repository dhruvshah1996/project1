1975

- 1978: TCP and IP split.
- 1980: Tim Bemer lee writes predecessor to WWW

- 1982: First public WAN initiated. Kilnam Chon connects Asia to the internet.

- 1983: After almost 10 years of refinement and bureaucratic haggling TCP/IP was adopted as the universal standard marking a milestone in the development of the internet. Paul Mockapetris invents Domain Name Systems.

- 1984: The first email arrives in Germany from the US.
